# AI Subject

This project, **AI subject**, aims to study the reliability and generalizability for using LLM-based responses to simulate human feedback in research scenario.

**Synthetic respondents** are AI-generated virtual personas used in market and UX research to simulate human feedback. Built on Large Language Models (LLMs) and trained on census, behavioral, and demographic data, they allow businesses to rapidly test products, ad campaigns, and pricing strategies without recruiting real people.

We further extend this concept to a wider range of research settings, such as survey questionnaires, open questions, and responses in experiments. Our goal is to study the reliability of this approach, and to investigate the benefits and drawbacks during generalization when the synthetic data is used.

## Questionnaire Respondents
Simulate the responses for Likert-scale questionnaires with LLMs.
